🏎️ How to Play

  • 🎥 Orbit Mode: Drag across the screen space with one finger or mouse to rotate around your vehicle.
  • 🖌️ Brush Mode: Select an explicit sizing from Circle or Square presets and slide your finger across panels to draw clean, seamless continuous lines.
  • 🪣 Bucket Mode: Tap over target segments to fill background panels instantly.
  • ⭐ Decal Mode: Drag over the car to position your decal in real-time. Use sliders to resize and rotate, then tap Stamp Decal to apply!
  • 🪞 Side Mirror: Set camera directly into Side perspective to open mirroring tools to replicate your design perfectly to the opposite side.
