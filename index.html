🏎️ How to Play

  • 🎥 Orbit Mode: Drag around screen space with one finger/mouse to rotate smoothly.
  • 🖌️ Brush Mode: Select an explicit sizing from Circle or Square presets and slide your finger across panels to draw clean continuous lines.
  • 🪣 Bucket Mode: Tap over target segments to fill background panels instantly.
  • ⭐ Decal Mode: Tap or drag on the car to lock in a preview position. Then, adjust size and rotation with the sliders, and tap ✅ APPLY DECAL!
  • 👀 Side Camera: Double tap the Side perspective button to quickly swap between the left and right sides of the car.
