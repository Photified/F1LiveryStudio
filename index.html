🏎️ How to Play

  • 🎥 Orbit / Free Mode: Drag around screen space with one finger/mouse button to rotate seamlessly around your racing chassis.
  • 🖌️ Brush Mode: Select an explicit layout sizing from either Circle or Square presets and draw across panels.
  • 🪣 Bucket Mode: Tap over target segments to fill background components instantly.
  • ⭐ Decal Mode: Pick target glyphs from the selections, adjust properties, and tap onto the frame to stamp.
  • 🪞 Side Mirror: Set camera directly into Side perspective to open mirroring tools to replicate patterns sideways.
